How SportIQ Works

Using advanced computer vision to provide real-time, human-like coaching feedback

🎥

Video Upload

Simply drag and drop your cricket video or browse to upload. Supports MP4, AVI, MOV, WEBM formats up to 50MB.

🦴

Pose Estimation

MediaPipe detects 33 body landmarks per frame — shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, ankles — with high precision.

🎨

Skeleton Overlay

Color-coded skeleton drawn on every frame — green for upper body, cyan for torso, orange for legs. Cricket-optimized visualization.

📊

Per-Joint Analysis

Every joint scored individually against professional reference form. Radar chart and progress bars show exactly where to improve.

🏏

Shot Detection

AI identifies your cricket shot type — cover drive, pull shot, sweep, defensive block — using joint angle pattern analysis.

💡

AI Coaching Tips

Get personalized improvement suggestions for each body joint. Tips are generated based on your specific deviations from ideal form.
