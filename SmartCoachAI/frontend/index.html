How SportIQ Works

Powered by MediaPipe and OpenCV for real-time body pose analysis

🎥

Video Upload

Simply drag and drop your cricket video or browse to upload. Supports MP4, AVI, MOV, WEBM formats up to 50MB.

🦴

Pose Estimation

MediaPipe detects 33 body landmarks per frame — shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, ankles — with high precision.

🎨

Skeleton Overlay

Color-coded skeleton drawn on every frame — green for upper body, cyan for torso, orange for legs. Cricket-optimized visualization.

📊

Keypoint Data

All keypoint coordinates exported as structured JSON — ready for analysis, comparison, and next semester's scoring engine.

📐

Angle Analysis

Calculates cricket-specific joint angles — elbow bend, knee flex, shoulder rotation — the foundation for technique scoring.

🔬

Side-by-Side View

Compare original and processed videos side by side. See exactly where the AI detected your body pose landmarks.
